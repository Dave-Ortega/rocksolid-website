About Rock Solid Estimating

Our Story

Rock Solid Estimating was founded as a specialized division of Colorado Fabrication Partners to provide expert estimating services to the countertop industry. We bring our parent company's extensive industry knowledge to every estimate, ensuring comprehensive and accurate project assessments.

Our team combines hands-on fabrication experience with advanced estimating software to provide detailed estimates that help you win more projects and maintain healthy profit margins. We understand the complexities of countertop projects from both a fabrication and installation perspective.

Our mission is to provide our clients with the tools and information they need to bid confidently and win more projects, backed by our commitment to accuracy, detail, and professional service.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive estimating services for both residential and commercial projects, ensuring accurate and detailed estimates for every bid.

Material Takeoffs

Precise calculations of required materials, including slab sizes, edge treatments, and special features. Our detailed takeoffs ensure you have the exact quantities needed for your project.

Detailed breakdowns of material costs, labor requirements, and project timelines. Our comprehensive cost analysis helps you understand all aspects of the project budget.
